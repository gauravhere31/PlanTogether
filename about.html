Vision

We believe group travel should empower local owners, not overwhelm travellers.

Plan Together is a startup focused on turning complex, multi-person trips into safe, delightful, and economically fair journeys for everyone involved.

Empowering local owners

We want local hotels, restaurants, and guides to plug directly into itineraries and budgets without losing control of their story or margins.

  • Transparent marketplace cards instead of dark patterns.
  • Clear schedules and capacity to avoid over-booking stress.
  • Revenue and feedback loops that small teams can actually use.

Scaling from first trips to global reach

The product is designed to grow: from small friend groups to operators, schools, and enterprises that need auditability and reliable safety rails.

Every component on this site is intentionally simple, composable, and API-ready. That is how we move fast now and stay stable later.
