Safety-first by design
Safety rails

Government-aware, traveller-first safety.

Plan Together is built to sit on top of public advisories, emergency services, and local infrastructure so every trip has a safety baseline.

Government collaboration

We are designing our data model to plug into government travel advisories, city open-data, and transit feeds where available.

  • Advisory-aligned city and region risk snapshots.
  • Embassy, consulate, and airport overlays for every trip.
  • Transparent fallbacks when data is temporarily unavailable.

Emergency POIs & offline guarantee

Critical points of interest 1 hospitals, police, medical clinics, and 24/7 pharmacies 1 are first-class citizens in the map layer.

  • Shortest safe routes to emergency POIs cached for offline use.
  • Late-night routing that avoids unlit or low-traffic corridors where data allows.
  • Printable emergency sheets for travellers who prefer paper backups.
Data & reliability

Secure by design, resilient by default.

Secure data storage

Trip data is scoped per group with strict separation for operators and partners. Future integrations will follow least-privilege principles.

Offline-first behaviour

Critical flows 1 itinerary, safety POIs, and navigation hints 1 are available even when connectivity is unstable.

Transparent system health

When third-party APIs or feeds are degraded, the UI clearly signals what is affected instead of failing silently.

Safety and reliability are core product surfaces, not add-ons.
